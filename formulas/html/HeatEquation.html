Heat Equation

The heat equation is used to calculate the change in temperature that will occur when a specific amount of thermal energy is transferred to or from a system. The change in temperature depends on the specific heat capacity of the material, which is the amount of heat energy required to raise the temperature of a substance per unit of mass.
